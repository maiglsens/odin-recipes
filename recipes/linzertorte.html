Linzertorte

This is my mum's amazing Linzertorte recipe

I honestly don't know what to say here, except: Try and be amazed ;-)

Ingredients

For the dough
  • 225g ground hazelnut
  • 200g sugar
  • 225g wheat flour
  • 225g butter
  • 2 tbsp Kirschwasser
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp drinking chocolate powder
  • 1 pinch backing powder
For the topping
  • 1 1/2 glasses raspberry jam
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients for the dough and knead well until you have a smooth and even consistency.

  2. Now wrap the dough in plastic wrap and put it in the refridgerator for a couple of hours.

  3. Preheat your oven to 180°C.

  4. Cut the cold dough in two halfes. Spread one half evenly inside a round springform pan and form a small edge around the sides.

  5. Now cover the cake base with the raspberry jam.

  6. Roll out the remaining half of the dough and cut it into stripes to lay on top of the raspberry jam forming a grid.

  7. Separate the egg yellow and whites and use the yellows to coat the dough grid.

  8. Put the cake in the oven and bake it at 180°C for about 35 minutes. Then turn off the heat and let the cake sit in the oven for another 7 minutes before taking it out.

  9. Let the cake cool off and you're done!

  10. Enjoy! :-)
